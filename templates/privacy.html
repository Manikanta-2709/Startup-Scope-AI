Privacy Policy

We only collect the information needed to run idea analysis, mentor booking, event registration, and account access.

What we store

Your account details, idea submissions, bookmarks, chat history, mentor booking requests, and event registrations are stored so your workspace can persist across visits.

How we use it

We use your data to generate analysis results, show your past validations, support follow-up chat, and process mentor or event actions that you request.

Who can access it

Your workspace data is intended for your account and authorized administrators managing the platform. We do not publish idea details to other users.

Retention and deletion

You can contact the team from the contact page to request account cleanup or data deletion support for this deployment.
